Respect

Define: Show respect towards your follow workers. Be nice and helpful to everyone.

How to implement: Everyone should get to know everyone else and learn how to respect them. Respect is a huge part of working hard together. Most people will work hard for people they respect and not very hard for people they donâ€™t like or respect. People need to learn how to control their emotions and work as hard as they can no matter the person. Everyone is different and have been through different things to make them who they are.

Why this is important: A business wants everyone to work as hard as they can no matter what. Holding back on how strongly you want to work costs the business money and makes you look bad. You always want to put your emotions towards others away while working. Working well with everyone is a huge part of being in a business.
