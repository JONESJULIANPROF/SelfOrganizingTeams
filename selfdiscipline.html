Self-discipline

Define: Pursuing what you believe is right. Knowing your weaknesses and working around them to better yourself and your team.

How to implement: Take some time in your groups to figure out everyone weaknesses and help them through them. Someone may not be good in one thing so have someone else step in and help them through it while they work on what they can do. Knowing your weaknesses is a huge benefit because it can make you accountable and trustworthy to people. It makes you trustworthy because you won’t say you can and will do something that is beyond your knowledge and power to do.

Why this is important: Knowing what you can and can’t do is very important in a business. If you say you can do something that you can’t you are losing the business money and time. You are basically taking away time for someone to do it right and costing the business money by having to go back and redo what you thought you could do.
