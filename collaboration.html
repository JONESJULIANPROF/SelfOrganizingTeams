Collaboration

Define: Working together with others to release and or, create a product.

How to implement: Learn how to work well with others. Teamwork is the main reason businesses flourish for years. The better the teamwork between people the fast and easier products will be made and produced. Even if you don’t like someone you have to push that aside for the greater good of the product you are working on and the business.

Why this is important: In a business they will not care if you don’t like someone if the group works together. Collaboration is such a big part of business because of the amount of money and time they can save by good teamwork. Money like always in a business is the end goal of everything.
